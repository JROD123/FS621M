Extra-large Volume

Developed with a focus on large build size 620 x 620mm with 1.1 meter Z height , Farsoon's FS621M featured one of the largest metal laser sintering build volumes in the additive market.

Productivity

The FS621M can be equipped with a powerful single 1000W laser or quad 500W lasers allowing for great rates of production. As a truly open platform,  FS621M  offers a high degree of control to tailor build parameters for cost-competitive metal additive manufacturing.

  • Powerful build process controls & real-time recoating monitoring.
  • Streamlined powder handling process shares a common powder container design which is used during loading, unloading, and sieving procedures.
  • Integrated filter module enables the exchange of filters without disturbing the build process.
